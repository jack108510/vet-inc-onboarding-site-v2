How It Works

Campaigns That Find Money You're Leaving on the Table

Each campaign targets a specific type of revenue loss and shows you exactly what to fix, what to charge, and how much you'll recover.
Campaign 1
Inflation Adjuster
Compounds CPI from each item's last price change. Shows which services have fallen behind and what they should cost now.
Real date tracking per item
Annual uplift calculated per SKU
Campaign 2
.99 Pricing Recovery
Rounds every non-.99 price up. Uses real transaction volume to show exactly how much revenue was left behind.
Based on real transaction history
Zero impact on patient perception
Campaign 3
Inventory Micro Margin
When supplier costs rise, the system adds an extra 5% margin bump on top of the pass-through price increase.
Auto-detects supplier cost changes

Revenue Growth

Shoppables — Exam Drop Initiative

Lower exam fees to attract new patients, then recover the gap by bumping pairing items (vaccines, diagnostics, treatments). The system calculates the exact trade-off — you gain volume, never lose money.
The Drop
Lower Exam Prices
Reduce exam fees to remove the barrier for new pet owners. The system shows which exams to drop and by how much, based on your local market and volume data.
The Bump
Pairing Price Recovery
Every exam pairs with 2–3 services. Small bumps on these high-attachment items recover the exam drop and then some — net positive from day one.
