How It Works

Three automated campaigns. Real dollar impact.

Each campaign runs continuously. No manual input. Real numbers from real clinic data.

Campaign 01

Inflation Adjuster

Tracks CPI from each item's last price change. Shows which services have fallen behind inflation and what they should cost today.

$864

Rabies vaccine — 18 months since last change. CPI suggests +$2.40. That's $864/year on one code.

Campaign 02

.99 Pricing Recovery

Flags every service code that doesn't end in .99. Calculates the gap per transaction and projects annual recovery based on real volume.

$9,400

47 codes flagged. Average gap of $0.51 per transaction. Annual recovery projected across all flagged items.

Campaign 03

Inventory Micro Margin

When supplier costs increase and you update your selling price, the system stacks an extra 5% margin on top — automatically.

$180

Detected 12 supplier increases in 90 days. Auto-margin stack adds ~$180/month in incremental revenue.
